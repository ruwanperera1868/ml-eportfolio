Unit 6 Project Proposal

The project proposal aimed to explore Airbnb pricing optimisation across New York City using machine learning. The proposed workflow included exploratory data analysis, regression modelling, and clustering to identify useful market segments. Key anticipated challenges included skewed price distributions, missing values, and the need to translate technical outputs into business recommendations.
